Our Story

Named for the stone that started it all.

A metate is the volcanic grinding stone Mexican cooks have used for centuries to turn corn and spice into something unforgettable. It is slow, it is by hand, and it is exactly how we still do it.

Every El Metate kitchen grinds, chops, and simmers from scratch daily, no shortcuts and no cans of close enough. It is the difference you taste in the first bite, and the reason our regulars keep making the drive.

The Menu

A journey of flavors

From regional classics to house specialties, here is a taste of what is waiting.

Sizzling Fajitas

Chicken, steak, or shrimp brought to the table loud on a cast-iron skillet with peppers & sweet onions.

Ceviche Fresco

Lime-marinated and served cold with tomato, onion, cucumber, cilantro & ripe avocado.

Street Tacos

Hand-pressed corn tortillas, cilantro & onion, your choice of asada, pastor, or pollo.

Chiles Rellenos

Roasted poblano peppers stuffed with cheese, lightly battered, and finished with warm ranchera sauce.

Salsa de Molcajete

Chile-roasted salsa ground fresh in the volcanic-stone molcajete, served with warm totopos.

Hand-Crafted Margaritas

Fresh lime, top-shelf tequila, a salted rim. The house pour that started the legend.
