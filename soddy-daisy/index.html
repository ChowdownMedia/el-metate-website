Our Kitchen

Guest Favorites

What Soddy-Daisy orders

Sizzling Fajitas

Chicken, steak, or shrimp brought to the table loud on a cast-iron skillet with peppers & sweet onions.

Ceviche Fresco

Lime-marinated and served cold with tomato, onion, cucumber, cilantro & ripe avocado.

Street Tacos

Hand-pressed corn tortillas, cilantro & onion, your choice of asada, pastor, or pollo.

Chiles Rellenos

Roasted poblano peppers stuffed with cheese, lightly battered, and finished with warm ranchera sauce.

Salsa de Molcajete

Chile-roasted salsa ground fresh in the volcanic-stone molcajete, served with warm totopos.

Hand-Crafted Margaritas

Fresh lime, top-shelf tequila, a salted rim. The house pour that started the legend.
